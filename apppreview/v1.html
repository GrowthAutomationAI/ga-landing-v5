Overview
PipelineFlow is a B2B SaaS platform that helps mid-market sales teams automate their outbound pipeline generation. We combine AI-powered lead scoring with multi-channel sequencing to help sales teams book 3x more qualified meetings without increasing headcount. Our platform integrates with Salesforce, HubSpot, and major email providers.
Features
  • AI Lead Scoring Engine that analyzes 50+ intent signals across the web
  • Multi-channel sequencing (email, LinkedIn, phone) with A/B testing
  • Real-time pipeline analytics dashboard with forecasting
  • Native CRM sync with bi-directional data flow
  • Automated meeting scheduling with calendar integration
  • Team performance benchmarking and coaching insights
ROI & Revenue Benefits
Customers typically see a 3x increase in qualified meetings booked within the first 90 days. Average deal cycle shortens by 22% due to better-qualified leads entering the pipeline. Teams using PipelineFlow report 40% higher win rates compared to manual prospecting methods. One enterprise customer attributed $2.4M in new pipeline to PipelineFlow in their first quarter.

Sales reps save an average of 12 hours per week on manual prospecting and data entry. Managers save 5 hours per week on reporting and pipeline reviews. Onboarding new reps takes 2 weeks instead of 6 with built-in playbooks and guided workflows.
