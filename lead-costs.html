KEY TAKEAWAYS

What the Data Shows

  • ▼
    Raw CPL is misleading. A $50 form fill that never converts is infinitely more expensive than a $189 guaranteed SQL. Always benchmark on cost per qualified lead.
  • 💰
    Ad platforms require additional spend. Google, LinkedIn, and Meta CPLs are on top of agency fees, creative costs, and landing page builds. GA's price is all-inclusive.
  • 📈
    MQL-to-SQL conversion kills ROI. With 5–18% conversion rates on ads, you need massive top-of-funnel volume to produce a handful of real opportunities.
  • ✓
    Guarantees change the math. Growth Automation is the only channel here that offers a contractual lead guarantee. No other platform or agency commits to a minimum output.
  • ⚡
    Cold email agencies are unpredictable. Retainer fees of $3K–$7K/mo with no volume commitments make cost-per-lead impossible to forecast.
  • 🎯
    Emerging platforms are not ready for B2B SMB. Reddit, TikTok, and X can supplement awareness, but none reliably produce sales-qualified leads at scale.
