Our Purpose

Awakening the Sacred Within

The Spiritual Wisdom School exists to bridge the ancient and the modern — offering seekers a grounded, rigorous, and deeply transformative path through the world's great spiritual traditions.

Here, education is not mere information. It is initiation. Each course, each practice, each gathering is designed to bring you closer to the luminous truth at the heart of all traditions: that wisdom is your birthright, and the sacred is alive within you.

Foundations

Three Pillars of the Path

Our teachings are rooted in three sacred principles that guide every course, workshop, and mentorship within the school.

◈

Wisdom

Deep study of mystical traditions spanning Hermetic philosophy, Tibetan Buddhism, plant medicine lineages, esoteric Christianity, and the Western Mystery tradition.

✿

Practice

Embodied practice through meditation, ritual, energy work, contemplative prayer, and sacred arts. Knowledge without practice remains incomplete.

Service

Your Guide

Krista

Founder & Lead Teacher

Krista brings decades of devoted study and practice across the world's great spiritual traditions. Her path has wound through Hermetic mysticism, Tibetan Buddhist training, plant medicine lineages, contemplative Christianity, and the Western Mystery tradition.

What makes her teaching extraordinary is not breadth alone, but depth — the lived experience of walking each path with reverence, rigor, and an open heart. She founded the Spiritual Wisdom School to create the sacred learning space she wished had existed: one that honors the ancient while speaking to the modern seeker.
